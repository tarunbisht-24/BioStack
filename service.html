Our Products

Our products can be used in various fields of applications like

Boilers

Our pellets and briquettes are perfect for use in boilers because they are an effective fuel source and have a high energy density.

Power Generation

Biomass pellets are used in power plants to produce electricity through combustion or co-firing with coal. Biomass power generation helps reduce carbon emissions and contributes to renewable energy production.

Cooking and Heating

In regions with limited access to modern energy sources, biomass pellets and briquettes are used for cooking and heating in households. They are a cleaner and more sustainable alter.

Animal Bedding

Biomass pellets made from materials like sawdust or straw are used as animal bedding in livestock operations. They provide a comfortable and absorbent bedding material that can be easily disposed of and replaced.

Waste Management

Biomass briquettes can be used to efficiently manage agricultural and forestry waste by compacting it into a dense, easy-to-transport form. This reduces waste disposal costs and environmental impacts.

Furnaces

Our products offer a dependable and sustainable fuel supply and can be used in furnaces for heating and other purposes.
